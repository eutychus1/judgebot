Case 1: Hiring Bot Bias

A company’s AI screens applicants, but rejects more female candidates with career gaps.

My Inspector’s Take — What’s Happening

The AI is acting as a gatekeeper. It’s trained on historical hiring data, and that data contains hidden biases. So, female applicants with career breaks get unfairly penalized.

My Top 5 Moves to Fix It

  1. Fairness-aware training: Retrain on balanced data representing diverse candidates.
  2. Blind sensitive fields: Remove gendered info before scoring resumes.
  3. Feature audit: Identify hidden proxies like gap length and adjust or drop them.
  4. Human review safety net: A recruiter reviews rejections to catch bias.
  5. Transparent metrics: Publish acceptance rates by gender to monitor fairness.

As the inspector, I’m in my detective coat shining a light on hidden hiring biases. Motto: “No ghost biases allowed!”
